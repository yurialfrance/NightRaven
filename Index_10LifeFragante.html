Night Raven College

Night Raven College is a prestigious four-year arcane academy dedicated to training its students to become skilled and talented magicians or sorcerers. Its doors are only open to students deemed to possess magical aptitude as determined by a magical artifact called the Dark Mirror. By enrollment, they can be assigned to one of the 7 Dormitories
