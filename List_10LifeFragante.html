  • 01

    Requirement 1

    Students are only allowed to enroll in this school if they have the potential for being a great magician, a factor which is determined by the Mirror of Darkness.

  • 02

    Requirement 2

    A black carriage will be sent to personally pick up incoming students, which can even travel underwater. They are kept unconscious in coffins called "Gates" until arriving at the academy. They can only be awoken if their Gate is unlocked with a special key, however there are exceptions. All students are given a magical pen upon enrollment.

  • 03

    Requirement 3

    There are fountain pens which hold the user's magic gemstones, and so are also utilized as wands.

  • 04

    Requirement 4

    The color of one's gemstone is determined by which dorm they are in.
